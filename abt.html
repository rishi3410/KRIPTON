About Us

KRiPTON is an online cryptocurrency simulator platform.

We aim at educating people of the volatile markets and teaching them to overcome it.

Key features of cryptocurrency simulators may include:

Virtual Portfolios: Users are given virtual money to create and manage cryptocurrency portfolios. They can simulate buying and selling various cryptocurrencies based on current market conditions.
Real-Time Market Data: Simulators often use real-time or near-real-time market data to provide users with an authentic trading experience. This includes live prices, historical data, and market trends.
Simulation of Trading Strategies: Users can test different trading strategies and investment approaches to understand how they might perform in a real market. This can include day trading, swing trading, or long-term investing.
Resources: Many simulators include educational resources, tutorials, and information about cryptocurrencies and trading strategies to help users improve their knowledge and skills. Performance Tracking: Users can monitor the performance of their virtual portfolios over time. This includes tracking profits, losses, and overall portfolio value.
